Princeton Python User Group

Who we are:

We're a group that meets on the second Monday of every month, usually around 7-8:30pm.

Whether you've never programmed, or have been programming for a long time - please join us!

The group is obviously Python-centric, but anything in the realm of open-source and programming is fair game too.

We're always looking for people to present, so if you have something you'd like to talk about, please let us know!

Tell us in advance and we can announce the topic beforehand, otherwise we always have an open floor for discussion.

There really isn't a high bar to participate; this is great space to bring up things like:

  • a project you've been working on
  • a problem you're stumped by
  • even something as simple as an interesting new library you've come across
